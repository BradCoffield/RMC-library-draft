Research Services

RMC Librarians are available and happy to help you conduct efficient, quality research. With decades of combined experience helping students one-on-one you shouldn't hesitate to reach out to us for help with anything research related.

Services Available

  • Research topic discussion (narrowing/broadening)
  • Search terms (keywords) generation
  • Help using library databases and catalog
  • Help identifying quality internet sources
  • Citing (MLA, APA, Chicago, and anything else you can throw at us!)
