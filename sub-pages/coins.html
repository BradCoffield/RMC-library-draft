Ancient Coin Collection

Our Ancient Coin Collection was generously donated to RMC Library by Jim Freeman, RMC Class of 1965. It is prominently displayed on the main floor of the library, next to our new books. It features coins from throughout ancient history, with coins representing the Byzantine, Seljuk, and Late Roman eras. They are able to be examined and handled and we encourage you to come and experience these material pieces of history!
